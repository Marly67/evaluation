History

The company "Dulces Rosse" has its beginnings in 1995 being its creator the T.S.U. in Alimentos Roselia Borrero since with her knowledge in pastries she wanted to make known to the population of Michelena the best typical sweets and cakes she made

Mission and vision

To be the best offering the richest sweets in the Michelena municipality and neighboring municipalities, thus satisfying the diverse tastes of the community to be the best in the municipality and at the regional level while promoting tourism.
